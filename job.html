Description

Our national firm Pan LLC seeks Tech Transactions & Data Privacy Associate Attorney with 2-5 years of experience to join our team located in Dragontown, Story Book.


Our Ideal candidate has to possess excellent academic credentials, experience in a law firm or similar environment, and superior writing, communication, and problem-solving skills.

Qualifications

  1. JD from a US ABA-accredited law school
  2. Admission to state Bar
  3. Experience must include transactional expertise in software, data, complex licensing, outsourcing legal experience
  4. Data privacy and data security law experience is highly preferred
  5. Team player with excellent communication skills
